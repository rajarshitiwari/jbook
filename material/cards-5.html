Alternative lists#

1

Discuss why should we use quantum computing software simulators when quantum hardware is available.
Answer

Accessibility, noiseless simulations, testing environment, learning and education, relatively low cost.

2

Discuss the differrences between tightly-integrated quantum systems and heterogenous quantum systems. Why might you prefer the use of one over the other?
Answer

Contrast and compare quantum stack component choices.

3

Discuss if you would choose a quantum computing stack based on your companies current connections with existing providers, or based on technical merits?
Answer

Open discussion.

4

Discuss and consider potential advantages and disadvantages in developing partnerships with publicly-funded institutions that provide quantum system access and expertise in quantum computing.
Answer

Example advantages: Low cost, access to academic research, and hardware resources. Example disadvantages: Public release of results.
